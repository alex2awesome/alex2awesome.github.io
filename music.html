  • Ukrainian Charity Concert #2 (3/26/2022)

    We organized this concert in Saint Mary's Cathedral in San Francisco, as a followup for the first charity concert we organized on 3/14/2022. (Unfortunately, no videos were recorded for the first concert). Shown here is a performance of Beethoven's 9th Symphony, transcribed for piano by Franz Liszt. I am playing the piano along with Lucas Spangher on cello and Svetlana Nikitenko singing soprano.

  • Personal Recordings 4/10/2022

    I had always wanted to play a version of Bizet's Carmen on piano, but, surprisingly, no canonical version exists. So, I transcribed my own. Here are recordings of two movements from the opera (the first is the "Habanera" and the second is the "Overture"). I transcribed them for piano solo, heavily inspired by the Anderson and Roe two-piano version.
